Volunteer Handbook
The Handbook is available for download for all WRC Volunteers. Simply click each section and save it to your computer or print it out. Check back often for updates or new sections.

  • Click on a section to download it.
  • Each section will open as a .pdf file. When the section opens, you may save it to your computer to view later, or you can print it out.
  • You must have Adobe Reader to download each section.
  • If you do not have Adobe Reader, click here to install it for free.
  • This Handbook is designed for use for all WRC volunteers.
  • All information contained within this Handbook is the property of the Wildlife Rehabilitation Clinic.
  • If you do not have access to print this Handbook, a paper copy can be purchased for a nominal fee. See your shift supervisor to order a copy.
  • Please check back periodically to download the latest version of each section.
